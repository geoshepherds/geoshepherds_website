ABOUT US

Our philosophy at Geoshepherds is to create data-driven visualisations that are ultimately designed with the end-user in mind.

Our motivation comes from a curiosity about data, the joy of working together as well as projects that challenge us and broaden our skillset. Through this, we hope to develop interactive and engaging experiences from data.
