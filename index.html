We help people convey information effectively by transforming data into graphics.

Our visualisations allow people to perceive hidden trends and gain insight into complex phenomena.

Through a data-driven and geospatial approach, we find meaning in data to help people tell engaging and informative stories.

We craft visual narratives that are ultimately designed with the end-user in mind.

OUR PROCESS

Research

We are curious about data.

Each project begins with a conversation with our clients where we establish their goals, target audience and define the focus of the narrative.

Data

We gather, review and analyse data.

This step lays the foundation of understanding the underlying patterns and relationships within the data. From this we are able to start thinking about structure and how we can convey a story while maintaining depth.

Concept

We begin conceptualising the visualisation.

Sketches and storyboards evolve into mock-ups and prototypes through feedback and dialogue with our clients. The goal of this step is to effectively design engaging user experiences for all relevant platforms and devices.

Development

We implement the design through code and graphics.

During this phase, we add functionality to form and make necessary modifications to the visualisation. We ensure that our design ultimately fulfills the goals established at the beginning of the project.

Delivery

We deliver a thoroughly tested and functional end-product.

It is our belief that the handover should be a smooth process where we are always available to our clients for support. This marks the beginning of what we hope to become an on-going collaboration of exciting projects in the future.
